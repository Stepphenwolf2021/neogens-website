01 — The Premise Neo-Generalist

Specialist and generalist. A restless multidisciplinarian, forever transforming.

We are tricksters who traverse multiple domains, living between the labels. The neo-generalist is curious, responsive and connective — bringing together diverse people and synthesising ideas across fields that rarely speak to one another.

Neo Gens applies that mindset to the big issues confronting organisations today: how to grow a business, how to teach and learn, how to put technology to humane use. We don't pick one lane. We connect them.
