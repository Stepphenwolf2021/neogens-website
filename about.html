About — The neo-generalist way

We live between
categories
and labels.

Neo Gens is built on a single conviction: the most important problems don't respect the boundaries of any one discipline — so neither do we.

See the practice →
01 — Who we areManifesto

Tricksters who traverse multiple domains.

The neo-generalist is both specialist and generalist — a restless multidisciplinarian who is forever learning. They encompass rather than reject; they bring together diverse people, synthesising ideas and practice to address the big issues that confront us.

That is the lens we bring to every engagement. We are comfortable in the spaces between fields, where most organisations feel lost and where the real opportunities tend to hide.

We are curious, responsive and connective — and we put those qualities to work for clients who need more than a single answer from a single expert.
